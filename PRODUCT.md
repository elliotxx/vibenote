# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Vibenote is for macOS users who capture thoughts, code snippets, logs, and drafts while they are already in flow. The primary user is a developer or creator who wants to write immediately without deciding on folders, files, tabs, syntax, or formatting first.

## Product Purpose

Vibenote provides one continuous plain text note stream separated by blocks. Success means the app opens into a focused writing surface, autosaves reliably, keeps block boundaries visible enough to navigate, and stays out of the user's way.

## Brand Personality

Immersive, effortless, AI Native. The interface should feel quiet, precise, and ready, with enough craft to feel intentional but not enough visual weight to interrupt note taking.

## Anti-references

Do not make it look like a SaaS dashboard, a folder-based knowledge base, a tabbed IDE, or a decorative AI product. Avoid directory trees, tabs, large toolbars, heavy green status bars, gradient AI effects, ornamental cards, and any UI that makes capture feel managed before it begins.

## Design Principles

- Open into writing, not organization.
- Treat blocks as rhythm, not containers.
- Keep AI-native affordances non-destructive and secondary.
- Make system state available without stealing attention.
- Prefer native-feeling controls over custom novelty.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast for text and essential controls. Focus states must be visible, color cannot be the only state indicator, and motion should be minimal enough not to disrupt sustained writing.
